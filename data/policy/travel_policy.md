# Corporate Travel Reimbursement Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Version:** 2.1  
**Applies To:** All full-time and contract employees

---

## 1. Eligible Expense Categories

The following categories of travel expenses are eligible for reimbursement:

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| **Airfare** | Economy-class tickets for domestic travel; economy/premium-economy for international travel (>6 hours). Business class requires VP-level pre-approval. |
| **Hotel** | Standard room at a 3-star or equivalent hotel. Suite upgrades are not reimbursable. |
| **Meals** | Reasonable meal expenses during travel days. Alcohol is NOT reimbursable. |
| **Local Transport / Cab** | Taxi, rideshare, or public transport between airport/station and hotel/office. |
| **Per Diem** | Flat daily allowance for incidental expenses (tips, snacks, minor supplies). |
| **Miscellaneous** | Conference fees, visa charges, travel insurance, baggage fees. Requires itemized receipts. |

---

## 2. Receipt Requirements

- **Receipts are MANDATORY** for any single expense above **₹500 (INR)** or **$10 (USD)**.
- Receipts must be legible, show the vendor name, date, and amount.
- Digital receipts (PDF, photo) are acceptable.
- For expenses below the receipt threshold, a written description is sufficient.
- **Missing receipts above the threshold will result in the claim being routed to Manual Review.**

---

## 3. Per-Category Limits

## 4. Duplicate Claim Policy

- **No duplicate claims allowed.** A duplicate is defined as a claim with the same employee ID, travel date, expense category, and amount.
- Duplicate claims will be **automatically rejected**.
- If an employee believes a legitimate duplicate exists (e.g., two separate cab rides on the same day for the same amount), they must provide separate receipts and a written justification.

---

## 5. Pre-Approval Requirements

- Claims exceeding **₹20,000 (INR)** or **$500 (USD)** for a single expense require **manager pre-approval** before travel.
- Claims without pre-approval for amounts above this threshold will be routed to **Manual Review**.

---

## 6. Submission Deadline

- Claims must be submitted within **30 days** of the travel end date.
- Claims submitted between **31–90 days** after travel will be flagged for **Manual Review** with a late-submission warning.
- Claims submitted **more than 90 days** after travel will be **automatically rejected** as stale.

---

## 7. Conditions Triggering Manual Review

A claim is routed to Manual Review when any of the following apply:

1. **Missing receipt** for an expense above the receipt threshold (₹500 / $10).
2. **Claimed amount exceeds 150%** of the category limit (flagged as potential overcharge).
3. **Date mismatch** between the claim date and the receipt date (>2 days difference).
4. **Vendor mismatch** — receipt vendor name does not match the expense description.
5. **Late submission** — claim submitted 31–90 days after travel.
6. **Pre-approval missing** for high-value claims (above ₹20,000 / $500).
7. **Unusual patterns** — e.g., multiple high-value claims in a short period, weekend travel without justification.
8. **Currency mismatch** — claimed currency does not match expected currency for the travel destination.

---

## 8. Partial Approval Guidelines

A claim may be **Partially Approved** when:

- The expense is legitimate but **exceeds the category limit**. The approved amount is capped at the limit; the excess is deducted.
- A receipt is provided but the **receipt amount is less than the claimed amount**. Only the receipt amount is approved.
- The expense category is eligible but a **sub-item is not covered** (e.g., alcohol on a meal receipt).

---

## 9. Rejection Criteria

A claim is **Rejected** when:

- It is a confirmed **duplicate** of an already-processed claim.
- The expense category is **not eligible** under this policy.
- The claim is submitted **more than 90 days** after travel (stale claim).
- **Fraudulent or falsified** receipts are detected.
- The amount is **zero or negative**.

---

## 10. Appeal Process

Employees may appeal a Reject or Partially Approve decision by contacting the Finance team within 15 business days of the decision. Appeals must include additional supporting documentation.
